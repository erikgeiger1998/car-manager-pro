Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your Car Manager Pro account at any time. Once your account is deleted, all associated data, including your uploaded car images and records, will be permanently removed from our Firebase database within 30 days.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the Car Manager Pro app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your deletion request.

If you are unable to access your account or the app, you can also request deletion manually by sending an email to:
