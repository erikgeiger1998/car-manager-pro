Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Car Manager Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your email address used to sign in via Firebase Authentication.
  • Vehicle data: Details you enter manually such as model, license plate, mileage, fuel records, and expenses.
  • Images: Photos of your vehicle uploaded by you, securely stored in Firebase Storage.
  • Technical data: Basic diagnostic and crash logs automatically collected by Firebase services to maintain app stability.

3. How We Use the Information

  • To provide and operate the core app features.
  • To authenticate and manage user accounts.
  • To securely store user-generated data and images.
  • To maintain app performance and fix errors.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services provided by Google Firebase:

  • Firebase Authentication – for login and user identity management.
  • Firebase Firestore – for storing user data such as vehicle and fuel information.
  • Firebase Storage – for hosting uploaded car images securely.

These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. For details, please refer to the official Firebase Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active or until you request deletion. Upon account deletion, all associated data (including car records and uploaded images) are permanently removed from our Firebase databases.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Car Manager Pro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or store data from children. If we become aware of such data, it will be deleted immediately.
